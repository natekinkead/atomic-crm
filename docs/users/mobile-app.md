# Mobile App

## Overview

Atomic CRM also comes as a mobile application, allowing you to access your CRM data and perform actions on the go.

## Installation

Atomic CRM is actually a Progressive Web Application (PWA). This means you can install it on your mobile device directly from your web browser.

To install:
1. Open your Atomic CRM instance in your mobile browser
2. Look for the "Share / Add to Home Screen" option in the browser menu
3. Follow the prompts to install the app on your device

After installation, open the app from your home screen and log in with your Atomic CRM credentials to access your data.

The app auto-updates whenever new features or improvements are released, so you always have the latest version without manual updates.

## Usage

The mobile app contains a subset of features available in the web application, optimized for mobile use.

Available features:
- View and manage contacts
- View and manage tasks
- View and manage notes
- Camera attachment for photos in notes
- Microphone for voice input

## Offline Mode

The mobile app supports offline mode, allowing access to data even without internet connection.

**Important note:** In offline mode, you can only access data that was previously loaded while online, including contacts, tasks, and notes.

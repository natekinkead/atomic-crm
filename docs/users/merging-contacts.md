# Merging Contacts

## Overview

Atomic CRM allows you to merge duplicate contacts into a single contact to maintain a clean and organized database.

## Selecting the Two Contacts to Merge

- On the Contacts page, select the contact you want to merge into another, then edit it
- In the Contact Edit page, click the "Merge with another contact" button on the side panel
- A dialog appears allowing you to search for the contact you want to merge with

**Key actions:** Select contact → Edit contact → Click "Merge with another contact" → Search for target contact

## Reviewing and Confirming the Merge

- The selected contact merges into the target contact
- All information transfers to the target contact; the selected contact gets deleted
- When both contacts have the same property (like position or company), Atomic CRM "keeps the one of the target contact"
- Click "Merge Contacts" button to complete the process
- All associated data gets consolidated into the target contact

## Causes of Duplicate Contacts

Duplicate contacts commonly arise from:

- Importing contacts from several CSV files that contain overlapping data
- Receiving an inbound email creates a new contact for a new email address instead of associating with an existing contact
- Manually adding contacts without checking for existing entries
